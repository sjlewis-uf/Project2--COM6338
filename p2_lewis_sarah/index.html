Welcome to the Pumpkin Patch!

About The Farm

Parker Farms dates back to 1941 and we put care into everything we do so you can enjoy spending time on the farm. When you visit the Pumpkin Patch, we want you to take a little piece of our family to make your own lasting memories.

Plan Your Visit

Walk through our orchards, pick your own pumpkin, and grab something sweet at our cafe while you're here! Whether you want to spend the day or just a quick visit, we have something for everyone.

Our Cafe

You may need a hot apple cider or a pumpkin spice latte to warm up with after walking the pumpkin patch or you want something sweet, like warm cinnamon sugar donuts, to carry with you before the walk!

Contact Us

We love making connections with the people who visit our farm and invite you to reach out to us before, or after, your visit. As you take pictures through the farm, tag us on social media to be featured in our posts too!
