Plan your visit to the Pumpkin Patch!

Whether you're planning a visit for the whole family, a day out for the girls, or bringing a special someone, we've got room for you at Parker Farms!

From locals to people passing through, we want to make sure you have all the information you need to enjoy your visit.

Old Family Tractor

Our family tractor is your family's tractor when you visit Parker Farms! The perfect spot for group pictures, selfies, and sipping your hot cider from the cafe. Don't forget to tag us!

Pumpkin Patch

What everyone comes to see, the Parker Farms Pumpkin Patch! Stroll through our acres of pumpkins and pick your very own perfect pumpkin to take home for the season.

Go A-Mazin'

Fun for kids, memories for adults, and even an adventure for your furry companion. The whole family can make your way through the twists and turns of our corn maze!
