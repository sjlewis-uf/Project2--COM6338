Stop by our cafe!

There's so much more to us than warm apple cider and perfectly prepared pumpkin spiced lattes.

Farm Fresh Pies

No pumpkin patch would be complete without homade pumpkin pie! We don't descriminate with our pies though. You can also take home apple and cherry pie by the slice or a whole pie for the entire family!

Caramel Dipped Apples

As they say, "An apple a day keeps the doctor away!" Our caramel apples are picked fresh from the orhard, dunked in caramel, and given a healthy sprinkle of chopped nuts lightly sprinkled with cinnamon sugar.

Baker's Dozen Donuts

Our freshly made donuts are still warm while you're deciding between classic pumpkin spice or apple cider! We recommend one for each hand. You can even take some to go and take them home with a baker's dozen!
